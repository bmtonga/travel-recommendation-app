OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2024, our platform began with a simple idea: to make travel planning easier and more personalized for everyone. What started as a small student project quickly grew into a powerful travel recommendation tool designed to help users discover unique destinations.

Over time, we improved our system by integrating smarter suggestions, better user experience, and real-time travel insights.
